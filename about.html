About our company

Posby is a swimwear company that stands for body positivity and comfortability. We want to create real expectations for our followers and brand supporters. We believe that every body type is beautiful and we accept everyone within our community; no matter what body type they have!

Our vision


“We believe that in a world where the media enforces a twisted reality about body standards to its audiences, it is very important to let people create a reality with correct expectations, because wrong impressions cause wrong expectations.”

Our values

Empowering

We want everyone to feel empowered and confident to be who they truly are. We believe that yourself is the most beautiful thing to be.

Honesty

We find it important to be honest about body image and we want to create a 100% honest reality about body image.

Respect

We find it very important to make people feel safe and feel respected within our community. We treat everyone with respect, no matter their looks or sizes.

Happiness

The most important thing that we wish for people is to be happy; happy with their looks and themselves. We believe that happiness is the way to confidence and self-acceptance.

Additional Information

Posby is a company created by students for a school project. We do not sell the products on our webpage, but we do stand in line with the mission of the company and we strive for body positivity and empowering.
